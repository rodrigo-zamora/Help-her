## Game information
#### How to play
keys:\
[p] for pause game\
[w] or [UpArrow] for move up\
[s] or [DownArrow]  for move Down\
[d] or [RightArrow]  for move Forward

#### How to win
You need to collect nine notes that they are into the doors around the map without lose your lives.
